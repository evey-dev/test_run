FINAL REPORT FIGURE INVENTORY
=============================

Purpose
-------

This file records the provenance and interpretation of every figure used in
`report/report.tex`. Use vector PDFs in the report. PNG files are previews and
SVG files are editable sources where available.

All listed figures have been generated and visually inspected. No GPU is needed
to regenerate figures from the retained JSON/CSV outputs.

Interpretation rules:

- Call it a compact force-associated panel or force-to-unit transfer panel.
- Do not call it a complete circuit or uniquely necessary feature set.
- The intervention swaps ten final-token latent values; it does not zero them.
- The top prediction remains joules.
- The reported 97.5% and 40.6% values are ratios of force-source intervention
  effects, not variance or mechanism explained.
- Six force-related decoder labels are supporting diagnostics only.
- Solid layer-28 paths are retained graph edges. The dotted path compresses
  unshown graph nodes.

The main report gives this figure a dedicated landscape page so all labels are
legible at normal zoom.

Final build and visual check
----------------------------

From `report/`:

  latexmk -pdf -interaction=nonstopmode -halt-on-error report.tex
  latexmk -pdf -interaction=nonstopmode -halt-on-error executive_summary.tex

Then verify:

1. No missing figure or citation warnings.
2. The landscape compact figure has no blank page before or after it.
3. Figure text is readable at normal zoom.
4. Captions preserve the no-token-flip and no-complete-circuit caveats.
5. Main report word count is <= 7,000 and executive summary is < 1,000.
